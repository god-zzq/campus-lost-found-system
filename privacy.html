Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address for account registration
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Item descriptions, photos, and location data
  • Contact information for claiming items

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate matching of lost and found items
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We may share information when:

  • You claim an item - your contact info is shared with the reporter
  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and theme settings
  • Maintain your login session
  • Understand how you use our service

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Item reports are retained for administrative purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and hosting. These services may have their own privacy policies.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our Help Center.
